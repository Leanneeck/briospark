My Story

I grew up in lovely Denver, CO. I graduated from the University of Colorado, Boulder, with degrees in Business Administration and Psychology. After working for two different roofing automation startups, I decided that I wanted to live somewhere different and challenging. I got my TEFL certification online, and I taught English in Ethiopia for 5 months. After Ethiopia, I moved to Austin to work on the Indeed VendorOps team.



My First Business

After a couple years of struggling with FAA regulations and looking for the right co-founder, I founded HoverStat to bring drone-powered aerial roof inspections and analytics to the solar, insurance, and architectual industries.





Skills

I learn eagerly and continuously. I'm comfortable wearing many hats, and I prefer a fast-paced environment where I'm challenged often. I'm experienced in product management, outsourcing, sales, social media marketing, business plans, pitch deck creation, pitching, partnership formation, Quickbooks, and 333 Exemptions. I've also dabbled in programming, and my current obsession is learning how to code through OneMonth.com. Check out my first complete Ruby app!
